Getting Started
Stop overthinking. Start operating.
1

Tap the link

That's it. It opens right in your browser. No download. No account. No email. No excuses. Just open it.

2

Set your 90-day mission

Who are you becoming? What are you building? What are you done tolerating? Write it down. Be specific. Be bold. Be honest. This is your identity for the next 90 days — own it.

3

Show up tomorrow. And the day after that.

Open it every morning. Plan your hours. Execute your power moves. Score yourself honestly. Log your wins, your trades, your prayers, your lessons. Do it for 90 days straight and watch what happens to your reality.

Add it to your home screen: iPhone — Safari → Share → "Add to Home Screen." Android — Chrome → three dots → "Add to Home Screen." Now it lives on your phone like a real app. Dark mode. No browser bars. Clean. Open it every morning like you mean it.
Your Data
Private. Automatic. Yours.

Everything saves automatically on your device. I can't see it. Nobody can see it. It's between you and your mission.

!

Back it up every Sunday

Your data lives in your browser. If you clear your data or switch phones, it's gone. So every Sunday: Settings → Export Backup. Takes 2 seconds. That file is your safety net. Anything ever happens — import it, you're right back.

The Code
The rules for the next 90 days.
01

No zero days. Ever.

Even on your worst day — open the app. Log one thing. One power move. One journal sentence. One score. The chain is everything. One zero day becomes two. Two becomes a week. A week becomes "I'll start over next month." Nah. Not us. Not this time.

02

Be brutally honest with yourself.

When you score your focus a 3, own it. When your trade was emotional, write that. When you skipped prayer, log it. This system only works if you tell it the truth. You're not performing for anyone. You're building yourself from the inside out.

03

Use the journals. Seriously.

Your trade journal will reveal patterns that are either costing you money or making you money. Your spiritual journal will show you growth you couldn't even feel in the moment. Your life journal is where breakthroughs get documented. Don't skip them. The people who write are the people who see clearly.

04

Trust the compound effect.

Day 3 feels pointless. Day 7 feels forced. Day 14 starts feeling like a habit. Day 30 feels like real momentum. Day 60 feels like a whole new identity. Day 90 — you won't even recognize the old version of yourself. But you have to get through the boring middle. That's where champions are built. That's where the magic actually happens.

05

Don't compare. Just execute.

This isn't a competition. Your 90 days are YOUR 90 days. Some people will fill every hour block. Some will just do the power moves. Doesn't matter. What matters is you're showing up when nobody's watching. Consistency beats intensity every single time.
